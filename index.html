About me

Hello again, my name is Maikel Iriarte, some call me mike!

I'm from Venezuela but currently living in Cuenca, Ecuador, a beautiful city in the mountains. I moved here about three years ago and love the weather and natural places it has!

Becoming a software engineer is something that started in me many years ago when I did not even realize it, it happened when I was 17 years old and all this Crypto world exploted to me, I was mining a crypto and I met a guy who was really into this technology, he was a trader and developer and was the person that inspired me to learn all I then did and I'm doing now!

I'm 23 now and a lot of time has gone, so what happened? The thing is that because of the Economy problems my country was going through I could not pay courses or anything to learn and I kind of stoppped because of that!

So when I finished the University 3 years ago I moved here to Ecuador looking for those opportunities to have the chance of learning!

All of this time here I've been working as an English Teacher and a cook! I've learned a lot doing this and I've had a lot of living experience, but I'm a builder, I feel that way, I like to create, to communicate, to bring to reality my imagination! That's whay I have made the desicion from some months ago to start this new journy to become a Web2 and web3 developer. Wish me luck!
